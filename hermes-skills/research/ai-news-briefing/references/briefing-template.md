## Tips for Each Section

### Top Headlines
- Lead with the most impactful story
- Each item: headline bold, 2-3 sentence summary, source link
- Include both technical and business impact dimensions
- Avoid items already in morning edition unless there's a major update

### Deep Dive
- Pick 1-2 stories that warrant more than 3 sentences
- Include specifics: model names, parameter counts, benchmark scores, dollar amounts
- Explain what changed today and what it implies for the field

### Tools & Releases
- Only include genuinely new tools (not existing products with new features)
- One-line description + link
- Skip if fewer than 2 genuine releases today

### Market & Trends
- Funding rounds, acquisitions, partnerships, strategic pivots
- Avoid pure price/ticker news unless it signals strategic shift
- 2-4 items max

### Quick Bytes
- 5-10 bullets maximum
- One sentence each
- Use emoji sparingly for scannability
- Can include regulatory, cultural, and infrastructure items

### Personal Insight
- Write for engineers, DevOps, and cloud architects specifically
- 2-3 concrete actionable takeaways
- Connect news to practical decisions (region selection, tool choice, governance)
- Keep opinion minimal, evidence maximal
